# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 15, 2026_

This is the short, honest version. Nyra is a small product — we collect the
minimum we need to run the service and we don't sell or rent your data.

## What we collect

- **Account information** — your email address, name (if you provide one),
  and the authentication identifier issued by our identity provider
  (Clerk). We never see or store your password.
- **Billing information** — purchase records and subscription state from
  Lemon Squeezy. Card details never reach our servers; Lemon Squeezy is
  the merchant of record and handles all payment data.
- **Usage analytics** — aggregate page-view counts via Plausible. No
  cookies, no fingerprinting, no individual user tracking. The dashboard
  shows numbers like "2,419 pageviews on /components this week," not
  "user X visited Y."
- **Error reports** — runtime errors are sent to Sentry to help us fix
  bugs. We strip PII before transmission.

## What we don't collect

- We do not use third-party advertising cookies.
- We do not track you across the web.
- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with marketers.

## Cookies

Nyra sets a small number of first-party cookies needed to keep you signed
in and to remember your theme preference. We do not use analytics cookies.

## Your rights

Email <a href="mailto:hello@nyra.dev">hello@nyra.dev</a> to export your
data, correct any field, or close your account. We respond within seven
business days.

## Children

Nyra is not directed at children under thirteen. We do not knowingly
collect data from anyone under that age.
