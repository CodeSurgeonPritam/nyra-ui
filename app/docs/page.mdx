# Introduction

**Nyra** is a React component library for developers who refuse to ship another
boring website. It pairs the breadth of shadcn/ui with the motion-driven aesthetic
of Aceternity, but with a deliberately distinctive visual identity — editorial
typography, electric accent colors, and original interactive effects.

The components here are **copy-paste**. There is no `<NyraProvider>`, no
mandatory CLI step, and no compile-time magic. Open a component, copy the file
into your project, install the listed dependencies, and you own it.

<Callout type="tip" title="The fastest path">
  Skim [Installation](/docs/installation), set up the design tokens from
  [Theming](/docs/theming), then go browse the [catalog](/components) and copy
  what you want.
</Callout>

## What's in the box

- **Free components** — every primitive on the catalog with `Pro` not in the badge.
- **Pro components** — full-page templates and the most ambitious motion pieces, unlocked with [Nyra Pro](/pricing).
- **Design tokens** — a small set of CSS variables (lime, ink, bone, rose) that drive every component. Override one and every component changes.
- **Original motion** — animations built on [Motion](https://motion.dev) (the Framer Motion successor). Spring-driven by default; no global keyframe collisions.

## A word on philosophy

> Every UI library on the market looks the same — purple gradients, Inter font,
> shadcn-clone cards. Nyra is the alternative for developers who want their
> work to stand out.

If you keep our visual identity intact, the components feel cohesive. If you
swap our lime for your brand color and our Instrument Serif for your typeface,
they feel like yours. Both paths are supported. Neither one requires fighting
the library.
